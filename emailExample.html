Garmin recently set a new record with its 10 millionth Connect IQ™ app download alongside the release of our newest running watch, Forerunner® 735XT. Boasting multisport features and wrist-based heart rate¹, this new Forerunner offers more for developers to work with. So, there’s plenty of opportunity to get creative with custom watch faces, data fields, and widgets that help inspire runners to “Beat Yesterday.”
 
We’re grateful to our growing family of third-party developers for helping us reach this historic milestone, and we’re excited to continue improving for our customers by rolling out Connect IQ 2.1. Chock-full of new capabilities for developers and consumers alike, it’s launching to developers in beta right now and will be available to the public in late July. So start thinking about what you’d like to create, and read on to find out what you can do with this update.

The Next Generation of Connect IQ
 
We designed Connect IQ 2.1 with the goal of giving developers even more ways to let their creativity flow when coming up with the next great app for Garmin devices. The new version contains:
 
 • Widget and app support for Edge® bike computers: expand your creations to the Garmin line of cycling computers
 • FIT recording: lets your metrics be displayed on Garmin Connect™, our online fitness community
 • New web APIs: allows your apps to communicate with authenticated web services
 • Much more!
